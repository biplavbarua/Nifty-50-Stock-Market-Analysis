# Predictive Analysis: Nifty 50 Stock Market
### INT234 Project Presentation

---

## 1. Introduction
*   **Topic:** Predictive Analysis of Indian Stock Market (Nifty 50).
*   **Goal:** Forecast prices and analyze trends using ML & Deep Learning.
*   **Dataset:** Historical Data (2000-2025) from Yahoo Finance.

---

## 2. Objectives
*   **Data Cleaning & EDA:** Trend Analysis.
*   **Price Prediction:** Regression Analysis.
*   **Buy/Sell Signal Classification:** SVM & Naive Bayes.
*   **Risk Segmentation:** Clustering Analysis.
*   **Time Series Forecasting:** LSTM Deep Learning.

---

## 3. Key Findings
*   **Trend:** Nifty 50 shows a consistent long-term uptrend despite short-term volatility.
*   **Regression:** Linear models are good for short-term but fail in crashes.
*   **Deep Learning:** LSTM models capture complex patterns better than traditional regression.

---

## 4. Methodology
*   **Tools:** Python, Pandas, Scikit-Learn, TensorFlow/Keras.
*   **Models:**
    *   Linear Regression (Baseline).
    *   SVM (Classification).
    *   K-Means (Clustering).
    *   LSTM (Deep Learning).

---

## 5. Conclusion
*   **Result:** Successfully built a robust predictive framework.
*   **Takeaway:** Combining traditional ML (for classification) with Deep Learning (for forecasting) yields the best results for financial analysis.
